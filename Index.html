How to calculate EMI?

EMI can be calculated using the formula: EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n/((1+r)^n-1) where P is the loan amount, r is the monthly interest rate, and n is the loan tenure in months.

What is a good BMI?

A healthy BMI range is between 18.5 and 24.9. Below 18.5 is underweight, 25-29.9 is overweight, and 30 or above is considered obese.

How is GST calculated?

GST is calculated by multiplying the original amount by the GST rate percentage. For example, if the amount is ₹1000 and GST rate is 18%, the GST amount would be ₹180.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to calculate EMI for a loan?
EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) can be calculated using our EMI calculator by entering the loan amount, interest rate, and loan tenure. The formula used is: EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n/((1+r)^n-1) where P is the loan amount, r is the monthly interest rate, and n is the loan tenure in months.
What is considered a healthy BMI range?
A healthy BMI (Body Mass Index) ranges from 18.5 to 24.9. Below 18.5 is considered underweight, 25 to 29.9 is overweight, and 30 or above is classified as obese. BMI is a screening tool but doesn't directly measure body fat.
How does the GST calculator work?
Our GST calculator works by taking the original amount and applying the GST rate percentage. It calculates both the GST amount and the total amount including GST. For example, for ₹1,000 at 18% GST, the GST amount is ₹180 and total is ₹1,180.
